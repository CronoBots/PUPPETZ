      Install the app

      Puppetz Leaderboards ranks the holders of the Puppetz NFT collections (crypto.com). Add the page to your home screen to use it like a real app — fullscreen, and even offline.

      On iPhone / iPad (Safari)

      1. Tap the Share button (square with an up arrow ↑) at the bottom of the screen.
      2. Scroll down and choose "Add to Home Screen".
      3. Tap Add in the top right.

      On Android (Chrome)

      1. Tap "Install now" above, or open the ⋮ menu.
      2. Choose "Install app" (or "Add to Home screen").
      3. Confirm with Install.
